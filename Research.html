My research intersts

I am a polar oceanographer, focusing on ocean flows at high latitudes. I am particularly interested in the role of the ocean in melting ice around Antarctica and Greenland. Studying ocean-ice interactions is essential if we hope to project future sea level. My current work focuses on modeling how icebergs decay into the ocean.
